A multi-service enterprise, filed and registered.

Orange Multimedia Global Konsult is a multi-service enterprise duly registered under the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), specialising in branding, ICT solutions, printing services, business support, digital services, consultancy and capacity development.

Mission

Operational excellence, for organisations and individuals alike.

To provide innovative, reliable, and affordable business support services that help organisations and individuals achieve operational excellence. Our aim is to become one of Nigeria's leading providers of integrated branding, ICT, and business support services.

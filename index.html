02 / Service Index

Nine services. All under one license.

A preview of the full index. Six of nine service lines, shown below.

  • 01 Corporate Branding We shape how you look and sound in public: logos, letterheads, complimentary cards, company profiles, seals, banners, branded wear and corporate gifting.
  • 02 Plastic ID Card & Lanyard Durable PVC identity cards for staff, students and one off events, finished with custom lanyards and card accessories.
  • 03 Graphic Design & Printing Flyers, posters, banners, brochures, certificates, calendars, invitations, product labels and social media artwork, designed and printed in house.
  • 04 Document Services Photocopying, full colour and black and white printing, document scanning, spiral binding and laminating for everyday paperwork.
  • 05 Online Registration & CAC JAMB, WAEC, NECO, NYSC and NIN registration, passport applications, CAC business and company filing, annual returns and general corporate documentation.
  • 06 Computer Accessories Keyboards, mice, flash and hard drives, routers, printers, toners and the networking gear that keeps an office running.
